┃  Analysis Pipeline:                                                     ┃
┃    Disease Modules (from Phase 2)                                       ┃
┃          ↓                                                               ┃
┃    Parameter Extraction                                                 ┃
┃      • β (transmission rate) = f(connectivity, variance)               ┃
┃      • γ (recovery rate) = f(redundancy)                               ┃
┃      • I₀ (initial infection) = f(fold_change, p-value)               ┃
┃      • Adjacency = co-expression network                               ┃
┃          ↓                                                               ┃
┃    Stochastic SIS Propagation (n_runs, n_steps)                         ┃
┃      • Transmission: β·A[i,j]·I[i] (binary event)                      ┃
┃      • Recovery: γ (binary event)                                       ┃
┃      • Persistence = infected_timesteps / total                         ┃
┃          ↓                                                               ┃
┃    Biomarker Validation                                                 ┃
┃      • Expression: CV > 0.5 AND FC > 1.5                               ┃
┃      • Clinical: p-value < 0.05                                        ┃
┃      • Literature: comparison with known genes                          ┃
┃          ↓                                                               ┃
┃    Prioritized Biomarker List with Scores                               ┃
